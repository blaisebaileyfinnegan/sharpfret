Guidelines
  • At the beginning of each week, pick a song to play a improvised guitar solo over.
  • Practice with it however you like, in an attempt to see what works and what doesn't.
  • Do not rehearse any part of the solo note-for-note.
  • Do not write anything down.
  • On the last day the week, record an improvised solo over it. This is known as staging day.
  • No editing allowed, except for trimming blank parts at the start and end. One take only.
  • Write an analysis of the overdubbed song, and of the process itself.
  • On completion, pick a new song and start over.
